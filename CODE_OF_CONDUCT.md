# Code of Conduct

Duggan Community League projects should be welcoming, practical, and respectful.

Participants are expected to:

- be kind and constructive,
- respect volunteers, members, staff, and community partners,
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks,
- protect private or sensitive information,
- raise safety concerns promptly and responsibly.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or access that violates these expectations.
