# Security Policy

Duggan Community League projects may handle sensitive community operations, so security reports should be handled carefully.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please do not open a public issue for suspected vulnerabilities involving secrets, authentication, member data, payment flows, emergency/status workflows, or production infrastructure.

Use GitHub private vulnerability reporting or contact an organization owner through an existing trusted Duggan Community League channel. Include:

- affected repository or service,
- what you observed,
- steps to reproduce if safe,
- likely impact,
- whether any data may have been exposed.

## Research Boundaries

- Do not access, export, modify, or delete private member, donor, volunteer, payment, or operational data.
- Do not perform denial-of-service testing.
- Do not send push notifications, email, SMS, social posts, or external account changes.
- Use local or staging systems whenever possible.
